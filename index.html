Our Story

Bridging Addis Ababa
to the Global Diaspora

"Carrying the heartbeat of Ethiopia to our community in DC, London, Dubai, and beyond."

Founded in the heart of Addis Ababa, Kidist Arsema Traditional Clothes was born from a deep reverence for Ethiopia's extraordinary textile heritage. We believe that traditional fashion is not merely clothing — it is identity, ceremony, and legacy woven into existence.

Each piece emerges from the hands of master artisans who have inherited generations of embroidery knowledge. Our tilets, our shammas, our Habesha Kemis — each carries the spirit of Ethiopia forward into the modern world without apology.

Ethiopian Heritage

A Culture Woven
Through Time

Ancient History
The Habesha Kemis Origins
The Habesha Kemis has its roots in the ancient Aksumite civilization, representing centuries of sophisticated textile culture in the highlands of Ethiopia.
Medieval Era
The Rise of Tilet Embroidery
The intricate tilet border embroidery emerged as a symbol of status and artistry, with regional variations developing distinct visual languages.
20th Century
Heritage in the Modern Age
As Ethiopia opened to the world, traditional garments became powerful symbols of cultural pride and national identity on the global stage.
Today
Kidist Arsema's Vision
We carry this legacy forward — honoring every thread of the past while crafting garments worthy of the Ethiopian century ahead.
